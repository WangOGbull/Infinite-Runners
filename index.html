How to Play

🏃 Control

Use the on-screen joystick or keyboard arrow keys to move your dragon around the arena.

⚡ Boost

Tap the Boost button or hold Shift to burst forward and escape enemies.

⚔️ Attack

Collide with smaller dragons to defeat them and absorb their power.

🛡️ Defend

Watch your lives! If you are larger than the enemy, you win the fight.

🐉 Select

Choose your dragon carefully. Each dragon has unique stats and special powers.

🔮 Upgrade

Earn coins in battle to upgrade your dragon's Defense, Speed, Rush, and Attack.
