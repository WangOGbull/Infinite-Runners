HOW TO PLAY

📱 Mobile: Move

Touch and drag anywhere to guide your dragon.

📱 Mobile: Boost

Tap and hold the BOOST button for speed burst.

🖥️ PC

Move=Mouse, Boost=Click/Space, Pause=ESC

⚔ Head vs Head

Longer dragon wins. Shorter shatters into ∞ collectibles.

⚡ Head vs Tail

Hitting tail cuts 20% from tail. Victim drops collectibles, slowed 0.7s.

🛡 Tail vs Tail

Nothing happens.

🪙 Collect ∞

Eat infinity symbols to grow. Start small, grow to max size.
