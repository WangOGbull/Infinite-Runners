How to Play

Desktop

Move your Mouse to steer the dragon. Hold Left Click or Space to boost.

Mobile / Touch

Use the virtual joystick (bottom-left) to steer. Press and hold the BOOST button (bottom-right) to accelerate.

Pause

Click the || button or press Escape to pause the game.

Head vs Head

When two dragon heads collide, the longer dragon survives. The shorter dragon dies and 60% of its segments become collectible infinity symbols.

Head vs Tail

If your head hits another dragon's tail (segment 8 or later), the tail owner loses 20% of segments from the tail and drops them as collectibles. Their speed is reduced by 50% for 0.7 seconds.

Tail vs Tail

Tail collisions have no effect. Dragons pass through each other's tails freely.

Collectibles

Infinity symbols scattered across the arena grow your dragon. Each collectible adds one segment, up to a maximum of 80.

Arena Boundary

The arena is circular. Going outside the boundary will bounce you back inside.

Survival Tips

Stay near the center of the arena where food is most abundant.

Use boost strategically to escape dangerous encounters or cut off opponents.

Circle smaller dragons with your tail to trap them into hitting it.

Avoid head-to-head collisions when you are shorter than your opponent.

When long, your tail becomes a powerful weapon that others fear.
